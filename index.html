THE HAPPY COUPLE

Our Story

Longin: From the first moment I saw Ruth, something inside me changed. She wasn't just beautiful—she had this calm, kind energy that made everything around her feel lighter. I told myself quietly, She's the one. But at that time, I knew the timing wasn't right. I didn't push or chase. I respected her space, waited patiently, and kept her in my prayers. I believed if it was meant to be, the moment would come. And it did. When I finally opened my heart to her, she looked at me with a gentle smile and said, I feel the same. That moment it felt like the world paused. From that day, my life found a new meaning. Now, we are walking together toward a beautiful future, and on 14 September 2025, we will stand side by side and say I do.

Ruth: He was so… silent. I hated it at first. I was used to boys who tried to impress me. Longin didn't even try. He just did the work—carried water, fixed broken things, stood in the background like he didn't matter. But somehow, I noticed him more than anyone. I tested him. I teased him. I wanted to know what he was hiding behind that calm face. No pressure. No flirting. Just care. And I knew—this wasn't a boy trying to win me. This was a man who was already giving himself. I started to fall for the way he listened. The way he never tried to change me. The way he looked at me like I was enough. Longin didn't say 'I love you' early. But every action, every silence, said it louder than words.
